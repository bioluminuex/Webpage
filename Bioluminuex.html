Digital Project

Wearable Healthcare Device Concentrating On Several Parameters

Our Focus

Our business model was created in early 2019 and got clearance by the endorsing body and the Govt. of UK
A year later our business model has expanded to serve patients having geriatric, palliative and chronic ailments within the UK
Moreover, we are planning to broaden our model to serve and help people
The initial idea of our model is to include only seven parameters monitoring adopted by NHS to provide emergency response to the local community
From here our team has widened the research and added on seventeen parameters to it, wherein our model 1.0 includes nine parameters for the first launch

Business Model

Our business model is a product-based service model that includes a wearable monitoring device designed under the combination of embedded techniques and software oriented working model
The model consist cluster of sensors integrated with AI and ML trained data models which in result provides clinical accurate data of a person’s health status
Eventually our model act as a digital tool for the doctors to support early intervention especially at circumstances wherein extensive diagnostic facilities are either limited or time consuming
Although our working model is a continuous active monitoring device, even without the wearable monitoring device (model) the software platform in our device plays a vital role hereby collecting and stores the recent data fed in it at a given point of time to corelate, analyse and provide vital information to doctors
Which helps them to find the major conditions or immediate intervention on some minor conditions

Business foresight

Our working model (wearable device) has larger scope in UK where the business organisation is positioned based on UK Govt
Approval while it can be expanded to several other countries where healthcare is governed and operated by government not limiting to EU, Middle East and many more countries
With vast opportunity around the globe, our mission is also to serve for the developing countries via United Nations which is the main custodian of health and welfare
There will be an exclusive launch in India based on wellness category and this will be explored via collaboration with reputed brand(s). With India being the second populous country, having the highest number of youngsters with busy lifestyle, our product can help them and their family to lead a healthy and safe lifestyle on a daily basis

Current Status

So far, we have successfully completed testing six parameters, remaining three will be completed by start of January, 2022
Our initial version of functional prototype to be ready by Jan 2022
After testing and corrections, our fully functional prototype to be released by May 2022
Our team is developing data model for nine parameters individually, combination of these can provide inputs for top 14 diseases in the UK, based on NHS statistics and 4 physical conditions namely geriatric, palliative, prenatal and physical disability under general health category
We have applied for intellectual property protection and currently under Provisional application and Prior Art Search in India. This will be extended to other regions of the world especially in the UK, Europe and MENA region before Dec. 2021
